About me

Welcome to my “Fall of Blacksburg” photo gallery. I am Esther Xiao, a communication studies student at Virginia Tech. I enjoy my life in Blacksburg and love to capture the precious moments I see with my camera. I believe photography is the best way to record life.

The website you are viewing is a project I did for my Visual Media class. The pictures on this website are taken in Blacksburg, Virginia with the subject of fall. There are various scenes: portrait, street view and natural scenery shots. These represent the different lifestyles in Blacksburg at many locations from campus to the Farmer’s Market.

For photography inquiries, contact me at xesther@vt.edu.
